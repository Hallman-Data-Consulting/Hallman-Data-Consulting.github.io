Business Sentiment Intelligence

Your customers are already telling you what they think — in Google reviews. This product listens to all of it, runs it through AI analysis, and tells you exactly what's driving your ratings: not just "positive" or "negative," but whether it's your wait times, your staff, your cleanliness, or your value.

The Fort Wayne demo tracks dozens of local businesses across restaurants, gyms, and auto shops. Any city, any industry.

  • Pulls and monitors Google Maps reviews automatically
  • AI identifies specific indicators — "long wait time," "rude staff," "always fresh"

  • See how you compare to competitors in your category
  • New reviews analyzed twice daily, dashboard always current
