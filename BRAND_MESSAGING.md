## Mission Statement

Hallman Data Consulting exists to give small businesses access to real, actionable insights by gathering, analyzing, and presenting their data in a way that drives confident, informed decision-making.

---

## The Problem We Solve

Most small businesses are sitting on more data than they realize, including data from things like POS systems, memberships, revenue reports, staffing records, and customer demographics. The problem isn't a lack of data, it's that turning that data into something genuienly useful takes time, money, and expertise.

Hiring a full-time data scientist is too expensive. Paying a large analytics firm gives you a generic dashboard that wasn't built with your business in mind. There's a gap between what small businesses need and what's actually available to them, and that's exactly where Hallman Data Consulting fits.

---

## What Makes Us Different

We work side by side with each client to understand the specific questions they're trying to answer, then build the analytics around those questions. Not a templated solution, but a genuine one. The goal is always the same: solve real business problems with your data.

---

## What We Do

We design and maintain custom analytics dashboards and data pipelines tailored to each client. Depending on your business, that might include:

- Revenue trends and sales analytics
- Product and program profitability analysis
- Staffing and operational insights
- Customer demographics and consumer clustering
- Customer sentiment analysis
- Predictive modeling and profitability simulations

If you're asking a question about your business and you think the answer might be hiding in your data, that's a conversation worth having.

---

## How It Works

Every engagement starts with a conversation about scope: what data you have, what questions you want answered, and what a useful dashboard looks like for your team. From there:

1. **Setup & analysis:** we build your custom dashboard and run the initial analysis. A one-time fee based on scope.
2. **Monthly maintenance:** your dashboards stay current with a simple monthly retainer. Pricing varies by client and data volume.
3. **New features:** as your business grows and new questions come up, we add to your dashboard for a small additional fee.

No surprises. Just clear pricing agreed on upfront.

---

## Voice & Tone Guidelines

- **Direct, not corporate.** Speak plainly. Avoid buzzwords and jargon. If a small business owner wouldn't say it, we shouldn't either.
- **Personal, not generic.** This is a consultancy built on relationships. Use "we work with you," not "our platform enables users to."
- **Confident, not boastful.** The work speaks for itself. State capabilities clearly without overselling.
- **Practical over theoretical.** Lead with real problems and real outcomes, not abstract data philosophy.

---

## Key Phrases to Use

- "Bridging the gap between your data and data-driven decisions"
- "Work side by side with each client"
- "Built for the specific questions your business is asking"
- "Your business's data"
- "Real business insights"
- "Genuinely solves real business problems"

## Phrases to Avoid

- "Leverage synergies" / "unlock value" / "empower your team": generic consulting filler
- "Enterprise-grade": wrong audience
- "AI-powered" as a selling point: lead with the outcome, not the technology
- Anything that sounds like it was written for a Fortune 500 company
